Mortgage rates depend on your credit score, down payment, loan term, and the type of mortgage you choose. A 30-year fixed rate offers predictable payments while adjustable-rate mortgages (ARMs) start lower but can increase.

How to Get the Best Rate

Improve your credit score above 740, save at least 20% down to avoid PMI, and shop around with at least 3-5 lenders. Rate locks protect you while the loan processes — typically 30-60 days.

Current Rate Trends

Rates fluctuate based on the Federal Reserve's decisions and economic conditions. Even a 0.5% difference can save thousands over the life of a $300,000 loan. Use our mortgage calculator below to compare monthly payments at different rates.
