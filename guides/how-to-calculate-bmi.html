BMI Calculator: What Your BMI Means

Learn what BMI is, how to calculate it, what the numbers mean, and its limitations. Free BMI calculator included.

calculate BMI, BMI meaning, BMI chart, what is healthy BMI, BMI calculator for adults

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared. While it doesn't measure body fat directly, it's a reliable screening tool for most people.

How to Calculate Your BMI

The BMI formula is weight (kg) ÷ height (m)². To make it easy, just use our free BMI calculator — enter your height and weight and get your result instantly.

BMI Ranges Explained

Under 18.5 = Underweight | 18.5-24.9 = Normal | 25-29.9 = Overweight | 30+ = Obese. A BMI in the normal range is associated with lower risk of heart disease, diabetes, and other health conditions. However, BMI is just one indicator — athletes with high muscle mass may have a high BMI but low body fat.

Limitations of BMI

BMI doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, body composition, or fat distribution. Athletes, pregnant women, and older adults may get inaccurate readings. For a more detailed assessment, try our body fat calculator or BMR calculator.

Healthy BMI Tips

If your BMI is outside the normal range, focus on sustainable changes: balanced diet, regular exercise (150 min/week), adequate sleep, and stress management. Small consistent changes lead to lasting results.
