Google Search Console

To verify ownership of usefultoolkit.org, add this site in Google Search Console and choose the Domain method (DNS TXT record) or URL prefix method (HTML file).

Steps:

  1. Go to Google Search Console
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "Add Property" and enter "usefultoolkit.org"
  4. Choose Domain method → copy the TXT record → add it to your domain DNS
  5. OR choose URL prefix → download the HTML file → place it at the root

Once verified, submit your sitemap: https://usefultoolkit.org/sitemap.xml
