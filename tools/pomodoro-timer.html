Pomodoro Timer

Boost productivity with focused 25-minute work sessions followed by 5-minute breaks.

⏰ Focus Time
25:00
Pomodoros completed: 0
📝 How to Use:
  1. Choose a task to work on
  2. Start the 25-min timer
  3. Work focused until it rings
  4. Take a 5-min break
  5. After 4 rounds, take 15-30 min break
💡 Pro Tip:

During breaks, step away from your screen. Stand up, stretch, or grab water. This resets your brain.
